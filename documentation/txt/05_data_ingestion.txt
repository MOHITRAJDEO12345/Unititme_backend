DATA INGESTION PIPELINE (EXCEL-TO-SUPABASE)
=========================================

To handle LPU-scale data (1,000 students), an automated ingestion service 
was developed.

SUPPORTED EXCEL SHEETS:
-----------------------
1. students: Basic profiles, BYOD status, and backlog history.
2. infrastructure: Building-room mapping, capacity, and equipment.
3. faculty: Teacher profiles and expertise tags.
4. courses: Subject codes, credits, and session durations.
5. student_enrollment: Mapping of Students to specific subjects.
6. teacher_subjects: Mapping of Teachers to their qualified subjects.

INGESTION RULES:
----------------
- Idempotency: Uses "Upsert" logic. Re-uploading the same ID updates the record 
  instead of creating a duplicate.
- Formatting: Automatic conversion of Boolean strings ("True" -> true) and 
  JSON strings (Array conversion).
- Security: Uses SUPABASE_SERVICE_ROLE_KEY to bypass RLS during bulk ingestion 
  to ensure 100% data integrity.

VALIDATION:
-----------
A diagnostic script `inspect_excel.ts` was implemented to verify Excel columns 
before ingestion starts.
