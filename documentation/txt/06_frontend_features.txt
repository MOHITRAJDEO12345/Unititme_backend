FRONTEND FEATURES & UI CAPABILITIES
==================================

The dashboard was redesigned for Personalized Scheduling.

1. PERSONALIZED VIEWS:
----------------------
- Student View: Enter StudentID (e.g. STU0001) to see exactly what classes 
  they must attend based on their enrollment.
- Teacher View: Enter TeacherName to see their daily lecture schedule.
- Room View: Enter RoomID (e.g. 25-102) to see utilization of that physical space.

2. CONSTRAINT MATRIX UI:
------------------------
- Admins can define global policies (Zero-Buffer Transit, BYOD Isolation).
- Visual feedback on conflict rates (e.g. 0.0% conflict achieved).

3. MASTER ANALYTICS:
--------------------
- Overview cards for total Student count, Room count, and Average Utilization.
- Real-time sync with Supabase `master_timetable` records.

TECHNOLOGY STACK:
-----------------
React, Vite, TailwindCSS (for utility styling), Lucide-React (Icons), 
and Supabase-JS.
